Municipality of Sasmuan

The Bamboo Gateway to the Coast

HISTORY

Sasmuan, formerly known as Sexmoan, is a coastal municipality in the province of Pampanga...

The town's history dates back to the late 16th century under Spanish colonial rule...

During the Philippine Revolution, Sasmuan played a significant role...

In the 20th century, Sasmuan's economy was primarily based on agriculture and aquaculture...

Today, Sasmuan is known for its unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty...

STORY BEHIND THE NAME

The name "Sasmuan" is derived from the Kapampangan words "Sasa" and "Pitabmuan," reflecting its historical role as a gathering point for soldiers...

TRADEMARK OF SASMUAN

Sasmuan is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, particularly its traditional delicacies...

INTERESTING FACTS

  • Sasmuan was formerly known as "Sexmoan," a name given by Spanish colonizers.
  • The town is home to the Santa Lucia Parish Church, one of the oldest in Pampanga.
  • Sasmuan played a significant role during the Philippine Revolution.
  • The town's economy is bolstered by agriculture and aquaculture industries.
  • Sasmuan is known for its traditional delicacies, which have gained international recognition.
