The name "Santa Rita" is derived from St. Rita of Cascia, reflecting the community's deep religious roots...

TRADEMARK OF SANTA RITA

Santa Rita is renowned for its agricultural products, particularly the delicacies "turrones de casoy" and "duman"...

INTERESTING FACTS

  • Santa Rita was officially separated from Porac in 1724 and achieved religious independence in 1771.
  • The Santa Rita de Cascia Parish Church, a historical landmark, was completed in the 1860s.
  • The town is a significant producer of agricultural products, contributing to the local and regional economy.
  • Santa Rita's cultural heritage is preserved through its festivals, historical sites, and community traditions.
