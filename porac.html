Municipality of Porac

Largest Town in Pampanga

HISTORY

Porac is one of the oldest towns in Pampanga, with a history dating back to pre-colonial times...

Porac is the largest municipality in Pampanga in terms of land area, covering about 314 square kilometers...

STORY BEHIND THE NAME

The name “Porac” comes from the word purac, a type of plant that once grew abundantly in the area...

TRADEMARK OF PORAC

Porac is best known for its quarry industry, especially after the 1991 Mt. Pinatubo eruption...

INTERESTING FACTS

  • Porac is the largest municipality in Pampanga in terms of land area.
  • The town suffered heavily during the Mt. Pinatubo eruption...
  • It has become a popular eco-adventure destination...
  • Porac was one of the first areas in Pampanga inhabited by Aetas...
  • The town is emerging as a new urban hub in Pampanga...
